About Us

Over the past one decade, Dr.P.K.ANAND has provided orthodontic education 2000 general , pediatric dentists and other dental specialists from world over. Participants in his programs often call him the finest orthodontic clinician teaching in this country today. During that time he has fined tuned his own Straight Wire technique, continually updating his methods by adding the latest and best innovations in dentistry. He has established practiced for 20 years.

His area of interest had been Fixed Orthodontics, Endodontics, dental Implantology and smile designing after graduating from the University of Calicut and the Govt Dental College Calicut in 1999. He first incorporated Straight Wire orthodontics into his practice in 2000 and has maintained meticulous records of all his cases, including yearly follow-ups. A highly enthusiastic presenter, Dr P.K.ANAND is motivating, as well as knowledgeable, enabling his students to return to their practice with a renewed excitement and enthusiasm for dentistry and their ability to contribute.

Program

Fixed Orthodontics

At Anand’s Dental Study Circle India, in orthodontics we provide 2 types of Fixed Orthodontics Programs for dentists who want to master in orthodontics, which are classified as:

  • Type-A :
    • Clinical Fixed orthodontics program-6 months long term / continious 12 days
  • Type-B :
    • 5 days short term basic fixed orthodontic program in MBT

Why are these programs special for general dentists?

  • 50 completed modules and batches. Over 850 dentists trained.

  • You get textbooks, CDs, study material, running notes, slides synopsis, videos.

  • You get typodont exercises, patient cases, pliers, and orthodontic material for hands-on practice.

  • Free consultancy in treatment planning, trouble shooting, online support & updates for an unlimited period through ADSC Google group.
